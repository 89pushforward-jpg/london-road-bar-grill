Beef worth

Our beef comes from Smithfield Market, London — then rests in our own dry-ager for up to 45 days before it ever meets the grill. Deeper flavour, perfect tenderness.

Behind it: seventy years of butchery and thirty years of hospitality. We know exactly which cut you should order — just ask.
